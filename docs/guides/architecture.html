BC2 Fabric Mirroring Architecture

Learn how BC2 Fabric mirrors Microsoft Business Central data into Microsoft Fabric to deliver governed, analytics-ready datasets for reporting and AI-driven insights.

Solution overview

BC2 Fabric provides a managed mirroring solution that continuously synchronizes operational data from Microsoft Business Central into the Microsoft Fabric lakehouse. The platform focuses on zero-code connectivity, automated schema handling, and end-to-end observability so finance and operations teams can build Power BI reports or semantic models without managing integration plumbing.

The mirroring process captures incremental changes from Business Central APIs, normalizes entities into Fabric tables, and surfaces curated shortcuts and warehouse views for downstream consumption. Built-in orchestration ensures the replication keeps pace with production workloads while preserving data quality and lineage.
